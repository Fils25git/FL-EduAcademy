About FL EduAcademy

FL EduAcademy is dedicated to providing top-quality educational resources for both teachers and learners in Rwanda. Our mission is to empower students with the tools and knowledge they need to succeed academically while supporting teachers with the resources they need to effectively educate.

Through our platform, teachers can create classes, upload educational resources like summary notes and schemes of work, collaborate with colleagues, and even set tests for their students. Learners can access these materials and take part in assessments, providing a comprehensive learning environment.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational platform in Rwanda, providing a comprehensive and dynamic approach to education that bridges the gap between teachers and students.

Our Mission

To enhance the quality of education by providing accessible resources for both teachers and learners, fostering collaboration, and enabling continuous learning and improvement.
